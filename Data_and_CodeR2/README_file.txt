These files can be used to replicate the analyses in Perez et al. Climate constrains photosynthetic strategies in Darwin’s Daisies: a test of the “climatic variability” and “jack-of-all-trades” hypotheses. 

Four files are included:
1) DEF_c85m20r_TreePL.pruned.tre- a phylogenetic tree from Fernández-Mazuecos, M., P. Vargas, R. A. McCauley, D. Monjas, A. Otero, J. A. Chaves, J. E. Guevara Andino, et al. 2020. The Radiation of Darwin’s Giant Daisies in the Galápagos Islands. Current Biology 30:4989-4998.e7.

2) Scalesia_Data_Mean_Climate_26Feb2022 - a datafile containing all of the data in our analyses that uses mean climate data.

3) Scalesia_Data_Median_Climate_26Feb2022.csv - a datafile containing all of the data in our analyses that uses median climate data

4) Scalesia_Script.R- A script used to perform the analyses in R
